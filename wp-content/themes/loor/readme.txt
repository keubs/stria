LOOR – Modern One Page E-commerce WordPress Theme

LOOR is a fresh and flexible one page e-commerce WordPress theme which brings enjoyable shopping experience. It’s ideally suitable for any kind of business to sell small amount of goods.

All in one: clean layout, page transitions, AJAX loading, smooth scrolling, lazy loading, social and SEO optimized. The theme is designed to offer a simple and intuitive process of customizing any layout elements.

LOOR has everything within reach to help you run a unique and thriving store starting from today.
